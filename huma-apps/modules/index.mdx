** Huma empowers Healthcare Institutions to build the experience and functionality they need, turning their Administrators into citizen developers. **

Each data category is contained within a module that has the specific functionality relating to what is being recorded. From here, a digital-first healthcare and research application is able to fit the requirements of a team and deliver value to Clinicians and their Patients, as well as all parties involved in a clinical trial.
